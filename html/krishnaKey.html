The Krishna Key

"The Krishna Key" is a thriller novel written by Indian author Ashwin Sanghi. The story is a blend of historical and mythological elements, exploring the mysteries and legends surrounding Lord Krishna, one of the most revered figures in Hindu mythology. Here's a general summary of "The Krishna Key":

The novel follows Ravi Mohan Saini, a historian who becomes the prime suspect in a series of gruesome murders. The killer is staging the murders to replicate the events from Lord Krishna's life, following a pattern that is linked to a mysterious artifact known as the "Krishna Key."

Ravi Saini must now embark on a quest to clear his name and unravel the secrets hidden within ancient scriptures and symbols. As he delves into historical texts and deciphers cryptic codes, he discovers a connection between Lord Krishna, historical figures, and a secret that could change the course of history.

The narrative weaves together elements of mythology, history, and modern-day thriller, taking the reader on a journey through time and mysteries. The characters grapple with ancient prophecies, hidden knowledge, and the quest for truth, all set against the backdrop of a gripping murder investigation.

"The Krishna Key" explores themes of faith, destiny, and the impact of historical events on the present day. It's known for its intricate plot, blending historical research with a fast-paced thriller narrative, making it a popular choice for readers interested in historical mysteries and mythological tales.
