Keepers of The Kalachakra

Keepers of the Kalachakra is a thriller novel written by Indian author Ashwin Sanghi. It is part of the Bharat Series, a collection of historical and mythological thrillers. Here's a summary of the book:

The story revolves around a brilliant but tormented scientist, Vijay Sundaram, who works for the Indian government's secretive intelligence agency. Dr. Sundaram is tasked with investigating a series of mysterious deaths related to a research project called the Kalachakraâ€” a set of ancient Buddhist scrolls with profound scientific and spiritual knowledge.

As Dr. Sundaram delves into the secrets of the Kalachakra, he discovers a complex web of conspiracies, ancient prophecies, and scientific advancements that challenge the boundaries between religion and technology. The narrative weaves together themes of history, mythology, and cutting-edge science as the characters race against time to prevent a global catastrophe.

Throughout the novel, Sanghi explores the interplay between science and spirituality, the consequences of unchecked technological advancements, and the timeless wisdom embedded in ancient texts. The characters navigate through political intrigue, espionage, and the hidden truths of the Kalachakra to uncover the ultimate mystery that could shape the destiny of humanity.

Keepers of the Kalachakra combines elements of mystery, suspense, and historical fiction, offering readers a thrilling and intellectually stimulating experience. Sanghi's storytelling incorporates elements of Indian history, mythology, and philosophy, creating a narrative that is both entertaining and thought-provoking.
